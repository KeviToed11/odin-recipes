EMPANDAS

Ingredientes for the stuff

  • meat: 1/2 of pork ground beef
  • 1 Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • olives
  • Eggs

Ingredientes for the layers

  • flour (1kg)
  • 500 ml of water
  • 1 egg
  • spoonful of oil

Steps: stuff

  1. chop the vegetables into dices, put some oil on a pot and cook them until golden brown

  2. Add the meat
  3. add the herbs, spices and seasonings
  4. Wait for it to cook
  5. Let it cooldown and then add some boiled eggs into tiny slices and th eolives

Steps: layers

  1. add the flour into a bowl with warm cup of water and a spon of oil
  2. mix it alll together,until its a firm dough that doesnt sticj
  3. let it rest for 10 minutes
  4. Grab tiny balls of the dough and use a roll pin to makle them flat
  5. Now add the stuff in the middle, close it and seal it.
  6. Put them on a non stick tray and cook in the oven at 220 c for 20 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
