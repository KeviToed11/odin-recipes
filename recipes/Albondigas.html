ALBONDIGAS

Ingredientes

  • meat: 1/2 of pork ground beef
  • 1 Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • 500 grs of gratted bread
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. chop the onion, garlic and tomatoes into tiny dices
  2. mix them on a bowl with the meat and the bread, and add one or two eggs
  3. make a dough with it and start making tiny balls with it
  4. heat some oil on a pan, flatten the meatballs and put them in the pan
  5. let it cook for around 20 minutes and then serve with rice, noodles or mashed potetoes
